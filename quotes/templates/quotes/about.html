About

Sun Tzu was a famous military general and strategist that lived from 544 BC to 496 BC. His most famous work, "The Art of War", a book detailing several military tactics (still used in today's military!), is still being read across the globe today. He fought during the Spring and Autumn Period.

The creator of this website is a big fan of Sun Tzu, and wants to share some of his quotes and pictures to whoever visits this website.
